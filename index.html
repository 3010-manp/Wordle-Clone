Wordle

How to Play
  • Guess the WORDLE in 6 tries.
  • Each Guess must be a 5 letter word.
  • Once 5 letters are added, then Hit Enter.
  • After each guess, color of the tiles would change depending upon whether the character was on right spot or not.
For Example:

W
Letter is in correct spot.
T
Letter is in the word but not in the correct spot.
S
Letter is not in the word at all.
